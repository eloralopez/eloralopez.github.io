About

I am a Biology Ph.D. candidate in the Palumbi Lab at Stanford University. My interests lie in the evolution of resilience, from the molecular level up to ecosystems.

Current Projects

Coral Bleaching

I study the resilience of coral populations that experience multiple bleaching events.

Genome Maintenance

I study genome maintenance in corals by measuring the load and patterns of their somatic mutations.

Ionizing Nuclear Radiation

I study the effects of nuclear testing on the genomes of wildlife.

Coral Germline

I am investigating the presence of dedicated germlines in scleractinian corals by determining if somatic mutations are heritable.
